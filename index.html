Why I Built This
The Context

Necessity is a good teacher. Working in an environment with limited tools and resources for design and cost estimation, the practical path forward was to build something from scratch.

These tools are the result of that, purpose-built for the specific workflows I encountered daily. They are not perfect, and they are still evolving. But they work, and they were built with intent.

This page is a quiet record of that effort, built at the close of a chapter, before the next one begins.

I am not proud because I made this. I am proud that I chose to find a solution rather than wait for one.
